Welcome

A message from a Past President

Welcome to the official website of the Illustrious Iota Gamma Chapter of Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority, Incorporated!

Since its chartering in the spring of 1974, the Illustrious Iota Gamma Chapter has been enriching and uplifting Northeastern University and the Greater Boston area through its stellar and captivating programming, community service initiatives and prime examples of sisterhood. 

We are excited to have the opportunity to continue this legacy by embracing the current international program initiative, Launching New Dimensions of Service, set forth by our International President Soror Dorothy Buckhanan Wilson.

In our pursuit to bring this vision of the sorority to our community, we hope that you will join us for signature and unique programs and activities in service to all mankind.

On behalf out our chapter, welcome! Please take this opportunity to explore our website and connect with us via social media.

Sincerely,

Jordane Perry-Smith
